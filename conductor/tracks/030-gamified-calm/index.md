# Track 030: Gamified Calm

## Overview
Incentivize calm physiological states through positive reinforcement.

## Objectives
- Define "Calm Streaks" based on sustained physiological baseline.
- Provide "Calm Coins" or similar rewards for navigating stressful times of day without spikes.
- Create a visual "Garden" or "Environment" that grows as the user remains calm.
- Integrate rewards into the watch face or a dedicated tile.
