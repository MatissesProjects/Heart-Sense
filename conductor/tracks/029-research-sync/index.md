# Track 029: Research Data

## Overview
Anonymized data sharing for Autism and physiological stress research.

## Objectives
- Implement a "Research Opt-In" system.
- Securely aggregate anonymized data (HR, HRV, tags, env triggers).
- Provide a mechanism to upload data to a central research repository.
- Ensure strict compliance with privacy standards (GDPR/HIPAA considerations).
